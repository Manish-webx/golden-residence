About the Project

Refined Luxury Living Redefined

Welcome to The Golden Residences — a premium living community located in the heart of IMT Manesar, designed entirely by Worldwide Realty. Spread across 172 acres, this landmark township offers fully furnished independent floors that seamlessly blend contemporary living with lush, green surroundings.

Thoughtfully crafted for families seeking space, comfort, and convenience, every floor features 3 bedrooms, a hall, and a modern kitchen — with the exclusive option of a private terrace or basement. Modern elevation, premium interiors, and a smart community infrastructure make it the perfect home in a fast-developing location.

At The Golden Residences, we believe every family deserves a home that reflects their aspirations. The exclusive clubhouse and integrated commercial zones ensure that everything you need — from leisure to lifestyle — is always within reach.

FAQ

Questions Answered

Where are The Golden Residences located?

The Golden Residences is located within the Golden City township in Sector 9, IMT Manesar, Gurugram. PIN code: 122052. Just 3 minutes from the IMT Manesar Industrial Hub and 5 minutes from NH-48.

Which floors are available?

Premium 3 BHK (1525 sq.ft) and 3.5 BHK (1980 sq.ft) spacious independent builder floors are available. Top floors come with a private terrace; lower floors include a basement for dedicated storage or parking.

Is The Golden Residences RERA certified?

Yes. The Golden Residences is HRERA Certified under Registration No. GGM/882/614/2024/109 dated 08.11.2024 — ensuring full transparency, legal compliance, and timely delivery.

What payment plans are available?

We offer a flexible 30:70 payment plan — pay 30% now and 70% at possession. Bank loan assistance is available through our tie-ups with Axis Bank and ICICI Bank. Easy, beneficial payment structure to suit your budget.

What is the price range & floor plan structure?

In plots with a basement: The first three floors get a dedicated storage room in the stilt, while the fourth floor enjoys private terrace access.

In plots without a basement: All floors are allotted exclusive terrace areas, offering open, private space for every resident.

Pricing starts at ₹1.41 Cr* at ₹9,700 per sq.ft.

When is the expected possession?

Possession is expected by June 2026. The project is HRERA certified (No. GGM/882/614/2024/109), ensuring legal oversight and adherence to the committed delivery timeline.
